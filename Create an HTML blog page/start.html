Nowadays, computers are used a lot. You can have multiple functions based on the use you make, for recreational and even work purposes. They are machines capable of performing complex mathematical calculations and data processing.

History

The first real computer was born thanks to Charles Babbage: his Analytical Engine, designed in 1833, even if never built, is considered the first example of a computer in history; it was a colossal machine with gears, powered by steam and equipped with inputs, outputs, memory units, decimal calculation units with data accumulation register and a connection system between the various parts and contrary to what is might think, it was entirely digital.

Description

The hardware components, generally enclosed within a container called a case, include the motherboard, the CPU, the power supply, the primary memory and the mass memory, plus possibly other cards responsible for accessory or complementary functions, such as one or more video cards or a sound card.
